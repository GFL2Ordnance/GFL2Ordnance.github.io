Intro

Hello there commanders o7, I'm Peipa the instructor of Ordnance at first Ordnance started from a very casual platoon which evolved under instructor Pei's management it happened because Pei wanted Ordnance to still be viable even on the harsh Change of Dark winter server competitive environment.

Maybe many people see Pei as being a try-hard but in reality, instructor Pei is just like you guys who wants an active platoon while also still wanting to have IRL time too. Since Pei has work that needs to be managed outside this game, there for this kind of Self-sufficient Platoon System has been created based on his experience. Everything has already been layout through a system that Pei calls rules either how many contributions or how long you can go off as long as you meet the requirements you can do it without worrying about being changed.

About Us

Ordnance is a part of Marshie Cozy Observatory Community, There are 4 GFL2 Platoons in there with 2 DW and 2 HAO. For Ordnance itself we are a platoon with a record Rank 59 on the last GS S5, a semi-competitive platoon with level 7, who focus on providing an active environment on the platoon to maintain stability for your troop to progress, also have friendly fellow commanders and commando to chat with.

We can promise you that our Platoon will be working based on mutual assistance, so everyone on this platoon will be active and move forward with you as long as you are active in the platoon. We also have multiple guilds on our discord community server besides gfl2 exilium and the server is also home to resident Vtubers. The server offers a friendly chatting English-speaking environment, weekly karaoke sessions, lots of fun events and even giveaways so don't miss out on the fun.
